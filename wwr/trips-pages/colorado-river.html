Colorado River Adventure

Rafting down the Colorado River is an exhilarating and breathtaking adventure that immerses you in nature's beauty. If you choose this journey, your abilities will be put to the test! This experience, which is considered one of our more demanding courses, will satisfy thrill seekers. Our trips down the Colorado are day long, pricing available on trips page.

History

The Colorado River, one of the major rivers in the southwestern United States, has a rich history dating back thousands of years. It served as a vital lifeline for various Native American cultures, including the Puebloans and Navajo, before becoming a crucial resource for early European explorers and American settlers. The river played a significant role in the development of the American West, supporting agriculture, providing water for growing cities, and becoming the backbone of the region's economy while also shaping the landscape through the formation of iconic landmarks like the Grand Canyon.
