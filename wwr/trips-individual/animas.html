History

The Animas River holds a rich history in Durango, Colorado, dating back to the town's early days in the late 19th century. Originally a lifeline for the local mining industry, the river facilitated the transportation of precious metals, playing a vital role in Durango's economic growth. Over the years, it evolved into a central hub for recreational activities, offering residents and visitors alike a serene environment for fishing, boating, and leisurely walks along its banks. Today, the Animas River stands as a symbol of Durango's natural beauty and serves as a testament to the town's deep connection with its surroundings.
